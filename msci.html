Highlighted Project

Refactoring the chart designer

The chart designer feature in BEON was highly utilized by our users, allowing them to generate custom charts from MSCI financial data. However, the older version of this tool was cumbersome and slow, prompting us to initiate a redesign.

Initially, we overhauled the feature's architecture and user flow to streamline chart creation. Through multiple iterations, we arrived at a version that satisfied all project stakeholders. Subsequently, we developed the user interface adhering to our design system and component library.

During development, we encountered performance challenges, necessitating adjustments to certain features. Although the final product deviated slightly from our initial vision, it significantly improved user experience, simplifying tasks for our users.
