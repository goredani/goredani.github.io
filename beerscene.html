BeerScene

BeerScene was born after a Startup Weekend in Copenhagen. Our goal was to show the best beers to beer enthusiasts in the town so they could find the best place to go.

Team
Designer, front-end developer.

BeerScene MVP

Tasks

  • Guerilla testing in beer bars
  • Create a prototype for testing purposes
  • Create the first MVP for the app

Conclusion
We released the MVP version which received a good feedback from the beer enthusiast community in Copenhagen. However the app was not scalable and later on we lost access for certain APIs which were vital to our service.
